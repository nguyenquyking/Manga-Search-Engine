Black Clover character lineup and story synopsis.  Individual character portraits include Asta (anti-magic), Yuno (wind magic),  Patry (light magic), Secre Swallowtail (Nero) (sealing magic), Devil (word soul magic), Licht (sword magic), Charlotte Roselei (briar magic), Lumiere Silvamillion Clover (light magic), Finral Roulacase (spatial magic), Yami Sukehiro (dark magic), Rill Boismortier (picture magic), and Charmy Pappitson (cotton and food magic).  The story depicts Asta and Yuno's journey to become the Wizard King, facing the resurrected Devil and his elf allies 500 years after the first Wizard King, Lumiere, was sealed.
