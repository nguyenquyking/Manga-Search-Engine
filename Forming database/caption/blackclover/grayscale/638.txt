One Piece manga afterword featuring four illustrated character portraits with accompanying text descriptions of their desires.  Captain Tabata wishes to use 100,000 yen wisely.  Comics editor Fujiwara wants camping equipment for a solo trip.  Editor Iwasaki desires an Aeron office chair.  An unnamed character wants a sushi chef to make sushi at home.  The afterword text describes the author's one-year-old daughter's contrasting behaviors at home and outside.
