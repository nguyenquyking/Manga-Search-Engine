This One Piece bonus material depicts Charmy in three different outfits and settings:  as a Native American, a cowgirl, and a matador.  The Native American Charmy holds a tomahawk and is joyfully exclaiming "La!". The cowgirl Charmy wields two pistols. The matador Charmy holds a cape depicting a roasted chicken leg.  The overall theme is Charmy's global culinary adventures.
