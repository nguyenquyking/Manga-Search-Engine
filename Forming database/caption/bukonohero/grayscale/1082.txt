My Hero Academia manga panel depicting a training exercise between Bakugou and Deku, observed by Shoto Todoroki, Ochaco Uraraka, and others.  Bakugou is frustrated about consistently losing in "Catch-a-Kacchan with Blackwhip," a training exercise where he must evade capture by Deku using his explosive quirk.  Sero, observing the exercise, is asked for advice on handling fast-moving targets due to the similarity of his quirk to Blackwhip. The scene emphasizes Bakugou's competitive nature and the strategic elements of their training.
