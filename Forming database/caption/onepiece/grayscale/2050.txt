One Piece manga panel depicting Solitaire, the Excavation Labor Camp Vice Warden, in the Officer's Tower at Udon.  Solitaire, a woman with multiple arms and the Monkey Smile Fruit ability, is enraged because someone stole a key to the Sea Prism Stone cuffs. She is shown confronting other guards, demanding to know who the thief is and expressing her intention to catch them.  The thief is described as having a "freakishly huge face." The scene takes place within the confines of the Udon prison.
