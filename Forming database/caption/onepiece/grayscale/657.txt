One Piece manga scene depicting Tama, a young girl, interacting with a sentient, reborn Climate Baton.  Tama is surprised and displeased by the weapon's ability to talk.  The Baton explains it was forcefully propelled into the weapon by Hera, whose soul had temporarily left its body.  The scene also shows the Baton reaching the stage, where the larger conflict is occurring, and expressing its inability to return to its original form without Tama's forgiveness.
