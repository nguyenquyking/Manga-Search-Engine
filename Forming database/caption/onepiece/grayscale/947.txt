One Piece 1000th episode commemorative cover featuring Monkey D. Luffy prominently in the center, giving a peace sign.  He wears a crown featuring Sun God Nika.  Surrounding Luffy are numerous One Piece characters, including Zoro, Sanji, and other Straw Hats, along with characters from other Weekly Shonen Jump manga.  The cover is a vibrant collage celebrating the milestone.
