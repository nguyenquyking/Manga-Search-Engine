One Piece SBS Question Corner featuring a hypothetical scenario where Straw Hat Pirates possess Devil Fruits.  Zoro is depicted with a Fish-Fish Fruit, Mythical Type, Azure Dragon Model; Nami with Rumble-Rumble Fruit; Usopp with Pocket-Pocket Fruit; Sanji with Swim-Swim Fruit; and Franky with Arms-Arms Fruit.
