One Piece Manga 1000th chapter celebratory page featuring congratulatory messages and illustrations from various manga artists: Ryo Nakama (High School Family: Kokosei Kazoku), Togo Goto (Phantom Seer), Kazu Kakazu (Our Blood Oath), Atsushi Nakamura (Agravity Boys), and Tomohiro Hasegawa (Moriking).  Each artist provides a unique illustration and short message congratulating Eiichiro Oda on the milestone.  The messages highlight the achievement and express admiration for Oda's work, particularly praising the Wano arc's art style.
