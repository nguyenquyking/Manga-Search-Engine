One Piece manga panel depicting a narrative about the Koziki clan.  The Shogun, distressed by the deaths of loyal daimyos in a seemingly civil war, falls ill from stress.  Simultaneously, Kurozumi plots to poison rival daimyos.  The Shogun's heir, Kozuki Sukiyaki, is born.  Kurozumi's plan is revealed, and Sukiyaki's grandfather is forced to commit seppuku, with the clan losing power, land, and titles.  The panels show the Shogun's distress, Kurozumi's malicious intent, the birth of Sukiyaki, and the aftermath of Kurozumi's failed coup.
