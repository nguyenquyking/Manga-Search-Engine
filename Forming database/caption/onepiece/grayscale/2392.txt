One Piece manga panel depicting a conversation about Oden Castle and Paradise Farm.  Characters shown include Luffy, a man resembling Zoro, and a woman.  Luffy is surprised to learn that all non-poisoned food in Wano is located at Paradise Farm, a large farm at the foot of a mountain, created by the late Lord Oden to feed the people.  The conversation reveals that Shogun Orochi controls Paradise Farm, using it to maintain his power over Wano's food supply. A young girl is shown sleeping peacefully, symbolizing the safety and abundance provided by the farm. The Kozuku clan's past rule of Wano is also mentioned.
