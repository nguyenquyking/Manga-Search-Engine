This One Piece manga page (Chapter 90) features character profiles for the Germa 66, Sun Pirates, Big Mom Pirates, and Firetank Pirates.  It includes portraits and descriptions of each character, including their names and familial relationships to Charlotte Linlin (Big Mom), such as Oven, Daifuku, Katakuri, Perospero, and many others.  The Germa 66 are represented by Yonji, Niji, Ichiji, Reiju and Judge.  The Sun Pirates include Wadatsumi, Aladdin, and Praline.  The Mink tribe members, Carrot and Pedro are also shown.  A brief story summary describes Luffy and the Straw Hats' confrontation with Big Mom, their alliance with Bege, and the ensuing escape.
