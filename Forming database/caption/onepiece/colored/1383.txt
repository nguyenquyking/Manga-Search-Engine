This One Piece manga page depicts Shirahoshi, Princess of the Ryugu Kingdom, a giant mermaid, heading to the Reverie.  She's fulfilling her late mother, Otohime's, wish for fish-men to live under the sun.  Neptune, the King of Fish-man Island, accompanies her, seeking amity between fish-men and humans.  The page also details the events leading up to this, including Luffy's promise to Shirahoshi and the conflict with the New Fish-man Pirates.  Otohime's sacrifice to protect a Celestial Dragon is highlighted.
