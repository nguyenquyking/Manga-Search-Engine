One Piece manga panel depicting the side effects of an artificial strengthening fruit.  Ten percent of those who eat it gain power; the other ninety percent lose the ability to express sadness or anger and can only laugh.  A group of Beast Pirates are shown consuming the fruit, with a large tank labeled "SAD" illustrating the consequences.  The "Waiters," who haven't eaten the fruit yet, are also depicted, representing a distinct category within Kaido's Beasts Pirates.  Franky and a woman are shown reacting to this information.
