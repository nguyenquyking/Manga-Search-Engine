One Piece SBS Question Corner featuring Oda's responses to fan questions.  The first question concerns the skirt of a character resembling Moda from the "Ace's Great Search for Blackbeard" arc. Oda confirms it is Moda.  The second question concerns the evolution of reader request chapter covers, originally featuring a Straw Hat and an animal. Oda explains he now chooses requests based on his personal interest, prioritizing novelty and creativity over strict adherence to previous guidelines.  Images of King Seki, Princess Comane, and various Straw Hats are included as visual references.
